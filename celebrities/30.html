Lisa (Lalisa Manobal)

The Global Dance Icon and K-Pop Superstar

:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}, known professionally as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, and dancer best known as a member of the global K-pop group BLACKPINK. Born in Buriram, Thailand, she showed a strong passion for dance from a very young age. She trained in various dance styles and later auditioned for YG Entertainment, where she became the only successful candidate among thousands. She moved to South Korea to begin her journey as a trainee, which eventually led to worldwide fame. ---

Rise to Fame

Lisa debuted as a member of BLACKPINK in 2016 under YG Entertainment. The group quickly gained international attention with their powerful performances and stylish image. Lisa stood out for her exceptional dance skills, stage presence, and charisma. She became one of the most recognizable faces in K-pop globally. ---

Music and Performance Style

Lisa is known for her sharp, powerful dance moves and confident rap delivery. She performs with high energy, precision, and strong stage presence. Her ability to switch between cute, elegant, and fierce concepts makes her performances unique. She is widely considered one of the best dancers in K-pop. ---

Solo Career and Global Influence

Lisa launched her solo career with the album *LALISA*, which broke multiple records worldwide. The title track became a global hit, showcasing her identity as a solo artist. She is also a global fashion icon, working with luxury brands and appearing in major campaigns. Her influence extends beyond music into fashion and culture. ---


Major Achievements

BLACKPINK Member — Global K-pop superstar group.

Solo Debut — Record-breaking album LALISA.

World-Class Dancer — Known for elite performance skills.

Fashion Icon — Ambassador for luxury brands.

Global Influence — One of the most followed K-pop artists.

The Legacy

Lisa’s legacy is defined by talent, discipline, and global cultural impact. She broke barriers as a Thai artist in the Korean entertainment industry and became an international icon. Her journey continues to inspire dancers and artists worldwide.
